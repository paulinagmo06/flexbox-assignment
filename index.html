About Sakura

Cherry blossoms, also known as sakura, bloom in the spring and symbolize renewal, hope, and the beauty of life.

Fun Facts

Cherry blossoms bloom for only 1–2 weeks each year.

In Japan, people celebrate by having picnics under the trees called Hanami.

They represent the fleeting nature of life.
